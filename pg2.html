About JB

Johor Bahru, often known as JB, is a vibrant city located at the southern tip of Malaysia. As the gateway to Singapore, it is a dynamic blend of modern development and rich cultural heritage. 🌆🛍️

Known for its diverse food scene, Johor Bahru has become a paradise for food lovers.🍴🤤

Beyond the city, Johor also offers a refreshing coastal escape at Desaru. Known for its long stretches of sandy beaches and peaceful atmosphere. 🌊🌴
