Local Food

Laksa Johor

Known for its rich and aromatic gravy made with ikan parang, served with spaghetti and fresh herbs. A true Johor pride!

Kway Teow Kia

A comforting local dish made with flat rice noodles in a light herbal broth, typically served with various braised items.

Hiap Joo Bakery

Beloved heritage bakery, famous for wood-fired banana cakes. Soft, fragrant, and worth the queue!
